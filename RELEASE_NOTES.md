# CodexPulse 0.1.0

First public build.

CodexPulse gives you a tiny menu bar indicator for your Codex rate limits, with the 5-hour and weekly windows available at a glance.

## Highlights

- See your 5-hour and weekly Codex quota remaining in the macOS menu bar.
- Choose the display style you prefer: both limits, 5-hour only, or weekly only.
- Open the dropdown for reset times, plan type, refresh, Launch at Login, and quit.
- Uses a compact CodexPulse icon in the top bar instead of text-heavy branding.
- Keeps working from the last successful reading if Codex is temporarily unavailable.
- Runs locally on your Mac and does not send your usage data anywhere.

## First Launch Note

This early build is ad-hoc signed, so macOS may ask for confirmation the first time you open it. If that happens, right-click `CodexPulse.app`, choose **Open**, then confirm.

Future releases can be Developer ID signed and notarized once signing credentials are available.
